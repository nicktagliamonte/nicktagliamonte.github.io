Books

An extremely long term project to read through a list of books.

The list describes itself better than I could in a short paragraph, but the central question is one of language and expressibility: what can be expressed via symbols, what are the limits of this expression, and why does meaning arise from them at all?

Try filtering by category to get a sense of general thru-lines. They contain the major rabbit-holes this project explores, as well as the deviations from the central question which I'm willing to tolerate (the chess books, for example, because I like to play. Or the items which deal less with the thesis of this project and look more like r/TrueLit's generic top-40).

About Me

I'm a Computer Science student with a background in IT helpdesk and an evolving focus on software development, data flow optimization, and project creation/conceptualization. I got started in development a few years ago, self-teaching some Java to create an application to reduce errors in new employee onboarding at a previous role in IT helpdesk.

After staying in the helpdesk field for a few years, I decided in late 2024 to dive in and pursue my dream of higher education and development. Long term, the goal is to finish this degree, pursue a masters, and eventually a PhD. For now, I'm looking to improve my skills in web development and am interested in internships or other opportunities for practical experience.

Personal Interests

Outside of development, I enjoy cooking and reading science fiction (Greg Egan is a favorite). Recently married, I share my home with my wife and a cat, Douglas (“Tiny Boy Dougie”), and have a bucket list goal to one day thru-hike the Pacific Crest Trail. I've been trying and failing for years to get good at cycling.
